# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Multiple barangay staff members operating in an office environment. They need to coordinate schedules, prevent double-bookings, and respond to resident inquiries quickly.

## Product Purpose
A centralized scheduling system for the barangay basketball court. It exists to replace manual logbooks or confusing spreadsheets with a clean, fast, and reliable digital tool. Success looks like zero booking conflicts and immediate clarity on court availability.

## Brand Personality
Premium, Modern, Aesthetic. The tone is professional yet highly approachable. It should feel like a state-of-the-art SaaS tool, elevating the daily work of the barangay staff.

## Anti-references
- Generic "AI slop" or heavily templated SaaS dashboards.
- The previous prototype: a claustrophobic, cream-and-red sea of empty spreadsheet cells.
- Clunky government portals with dense bureaucratic UI.

## Design Principles
- **Clarity over density:** Surface what needs attention right now (today's schedule, pending requests) rather than overwhelming the user with empty weekly grids.
- **Premium utility:** Elevate everyday tasks. Even a simple scheduling tool deserves high-craft interactions and polished aesthetics.
- **Unmistakable actions:** Destructive actions (like canceling) and constructive actions (reserving) must be structurally and visually distinct.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast and clear typography.
- Fully accessible for varying levels of digital literacy among staff.
- Keyboard navigable for efficient data entry.
